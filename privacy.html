Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 5, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to DevToolBox ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, use, and protection when you use our website and tools.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

Our tools run entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any data you enter into our tools. All processing happens locally on your device, and your data never leaves your browser.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • Referring website

2.3 Cookies and Local Storage

We use local storage to save your theme preference (dark/light mode). This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

We may use cookies for analytics and advertising purposes through third-party services like Google Analytics and Google AdSense.

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google may use cookies and web beacons to collect information about your visits to this and other websites to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

3.2 Google Analytics

We may use Google Analytics to analyze website traffic and usage patterns. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymous information about how visitors use our site.

4. How We Use Information

We use the automatically collected information to:

  • Improve our website and tools
  • Understand user behavior and preferences
  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Analyze website performance
  • Detect and prevent abuse

5. Data Security

Since our tools run entirely in your browser and we don't collect your tool usage data, your information remains on your device. We implement reasonable security measures to protect any information we do collect (such as analytics data).

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Disable cookies in your browser settings

7. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@devtoolbox.com
Website: Contact Page

10. GDPR Compliance

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain data protection rights. We aim to take reasonable steps to allow you to correct, amend, delete, or limit the use of your personal data.

11. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights regarding your personal information under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). You have the right to request information about the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you.
